I do not think there is any thrill that can go through the human heart like that felt by the inventor as he sees some creation of the brain unfolding to success…
Such emotions make a man forget food, sleep, friends, love, everything.

As a kid, I loved to play lego's, puzzles and build constructions. Some time ago I've decided to give a try and learn some programming. I felt in love at the first glance in CSS and Javascipt. Making things out of nothing brings me enourmous ammount of joy and satisfaction. This is definetly THAT THING I want to do my whole life.

First step is to find a job as a Junior Frontend Developer, and here is where I'm looking for some help from your side. If you want to know how I can help you, just jump in here.

WHAT AM I GOOD AT?

A cultural anthropologist with a soul of engineer, that's me. Those interdisciplinary studies prepared me to easily explore even the most hidden areas of our culture. My promoter used to say that I have an unique ability of synthetical thinking.

I percieve things as a system, an entirety in widest possible context. As the culture, especially our modern culture, is built of signs and symbols, I can recognize them very quickly and arrange in various configurations. This ability can be very useful in working with logotypes and creating meaningful web design.

Would you like to give me a try?

I have superpowers in research. Being curious about some hidden information, I'll dig the internet through like a dachshund down the rabbit hole.

Are you looking for something special?

I love experiments! To search for new solutions, watch them growing, try them, test them and implement. To seek inspiration where nobody tried before. I'd love to take care of your garden of ideas. Science is fun! Let me be your Nikola Tesla...

Quality is important to me. No matter what I do, I always do my best to make sure that there are no gaps in the system. I used to visualize the craziest possible threats to check if my project has any points of weakness. Asking questions and critical thinking is the clue! Let me be your Lieutenant Colombo...

My specialty of the house is “making something out of nothing”. I use my wild creativity to face difficult situations. I believe that the internet gave us enough tools to successfully face any problem in a charming way. Because there is always a way. Let me be your Angus MacGyver...
